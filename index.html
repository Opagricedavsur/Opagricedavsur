Mission & Vision

Mission

OPAG serves as catalyst for agricultural development through the provision of quality extension services and technology transfer to farmers and fisherfolk in the province.

Vision

OPAG envisions a dynamic, self-reliant and sustainable agricultural sector that contributes to food security, poverty alleviation and rural development.

Mandate

OPAG is the lead agency of the Provincial Local Government Unit responsible for providing agricultural extension services, technology transfer, and other support services to farmers and fisherfolk in the province.
