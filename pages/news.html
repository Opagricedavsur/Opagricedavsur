  • Davao del Sur's PRTF Advances Rice Tech Collaboration

    July 15, 2025

    The Provincial Rice Technology Forum (PRTF) 2025, organized by the Office of the Provincial Agriculturist (OPAG), successfully concluded, bringing together public and private sector stakeholders. The forum focused on aligning plans for upcoming rice technology demonstrations. Key outcomes include finalized cost-sharing agreements, commitments for soil analysis by biofertilizer companies, and the establishment of clearer communication channels via an official group chat. The event highlights Davao del Sur's dedication to boosting rice productivity through collaborative efforts, research integration, and efficient planning.
