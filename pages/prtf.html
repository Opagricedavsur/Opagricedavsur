About the Provincial Rice Technology Forum (PRTF)

Davao del Sur

The Provincial Rice Technology Forum (PRTF) is a joint initiative of the Provincial Government of Davao del Sur through OPAG, in partnership with DA-RFO XI, NIA, PhilRice, ATI-XI, LGUs, and private stakeholders. It is a season-long field demonstration platform designed to showcase and evaluate modern rice technologies to boost productivity and sustainability in rice farming.

  • Complete documentation, monitored applications, and crop-cutting
